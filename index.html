Privacy Policy for Community Collection

Community Collection helps community groups manage monthly collections, member payments, approvals, reports, and treasury records.

Information We Collect

We may collect member name, mobile number, group membership details, payment records, payment screenshots, and admin-entered treasury or expense information.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to manage group collections, show payment history, support admin approvals, and maintain reports.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data is only visible to authorized group admins and members as required by the app features.
